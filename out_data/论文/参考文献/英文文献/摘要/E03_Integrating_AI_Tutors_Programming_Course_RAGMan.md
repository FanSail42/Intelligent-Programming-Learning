## 摘要

RAGMan is an LLM-powered tutoring system supporting course-specific and homework-specific AI tutors using Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) and strict instructions. Deployed optionally in an introductory programming course (455 students) as five homework-specific tutors, about half of students engaged; within intended scope, tutors delivered accurate responses 98% of the time, and 78% of users reported the tutors helped their learning.
