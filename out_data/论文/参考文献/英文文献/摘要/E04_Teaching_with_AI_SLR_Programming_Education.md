## 摘要

This review analyzes 58 peer-reviewed studies (2022–2025) on AI agents in programming education, categorizing them as chatbots, generative AI, and intelligent tutoring systems. Primary objectives include enhanced programming support (94.83%), motivational benefits (18.96%), and educator efficiency (6.90%). Challenges include setup barriers (93.10%), overreliance and superficial learning (65.52%), and concerns about AI errors and academic integrity.
