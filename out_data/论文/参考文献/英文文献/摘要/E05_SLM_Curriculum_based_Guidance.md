## 摘要

This study develops AI teaching assistants providing curriculum-based guidance via a RAG pipeline over open-source small language models (LLaMA 3.1, IBM Granite 3.3, Gemma 3, etc.), benchmarked against GPT-4o. With proper prompting and targeted retrieval, SLMs can match LLMs in pedagogically aligned responses while offering lower cost, privacy preservation, and sustainable local deployment on consumer hardware.
